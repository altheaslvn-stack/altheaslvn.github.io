01About

One assistant, four areas of real expertise

I help healthcare providers, e-commerce brands, and busy teams run smoother — from the clinical back office to the storefront. Over the past three-plus years I've moved fluidly between medical billing and revenue cycle management, customer support and consultative sales, Shopify store administration, and day-to-day administrative support, and I've learned that each of these disciplines shares the same foundation: accuracy, organization, and clear communication.

On the healthcare side, I support insurance verification, Medicare and Medicaid eligibility checks, claims processing, payment posting, and denial follow-up, always with HIPAA compliance and clean documentation at the center of the work. In customer-facing roles, I combine active listening with a consultative sales approach to resolve issues and grow accounts. As a Shopify Virtual Assistant, I keep storefronts accurate and SEO-ready, and as an administrative and CRM specialist, I keep calendars, inboxes, and pipelines organized so nothing slips through.

Whatever the task, I bring the same habits: proactive updates, tidy systems, and a genuine interest in making the business run better than it did yesterday.
